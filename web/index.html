Global context

The data on this map directly contributes to two of Earth's nine planetary boundaries currently beyond their safe limits.

Radiative Forcing At risk
~2.9 W/m² observed · safe boundary: 1.0 W/m² — driven by CO₂ from fossil fuels
Biosphere Integrity (HANPP) Exceeded
>75% HANPP observed · safe boundary: <25% — driven by land use change and deforestation
